  • The address of the hotel is Lane 258, Lv Hu Road, Qingpu District, Shanghai (上海市青浦区绿湖路 258 弄). The official website is http://www.orientluzhou.com/.
  • The length of each paper presentation will be 15 minutes, including time for Q&A. Posters should be 80cm (31 in) in width and 110cm (43 in) in height (no higher than 180cm (70 in)).

WELCOME TO IALP 2019!

The 2019 International Conference on Asian Language Processing (IALP 2019) will be held in Shanghai, China on 15 - 17 November 2019. The event is organized by East China Normal University and Chinese and Oriental Languages Information Processing Society (COLIPS), and is technically sponsored by IEEE Singapore SMC Chapter.
