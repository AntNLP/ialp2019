Shanghai Oriental Land Hotel

Arrivals

Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport

  • Metro (1 hour, 6 RMB)
    • Take Metro Line 10 from the Hongqiao Airport Terminal 1/2 to it's terminal station, Hongqiao Railway Station, then transfer Metro Line 17 to Oriental Land station, the terminal station too.
  • Taxi (45+ mins, 126+ RMB)
    • Call a taxi from the Airport to Oriental Land Hotel.

Shanghai Pudong International Airport

  • Metro (2.5 hours, 12 RMB)
    • Take Metro Line 2 from the Airport to it's terminal station, Hongqiao Railway Station, then transfer Metro Line 17 to Oriental Land station, the terminal station too.
  • Maglev (2 hours, 60 RMB)
    • Take the maglev train from the Airport to Longyang Road and transfer Metro Line 2 to it's terminal station, Hongqiao Railway Station. Then transfer Metro Line 17 to Oriental Land station.
  • Taxi (100+ mins, 300+ RMB)
    • Call a taxi from the Airport to Oriental Land Hotel.

Shanghai Railway Station

  • Metro (100 mins, 8 RMB)
    • Take Metro Line 3 or 4 from the Station and transfer to Line 2 at Zhongshan Park station. Then transfer to Line 17 at Hongqiao Railway Station, the terminal station of Line 2. Take Metro Line 17 to its terminal station, Oriental Land station.
  • Taxi (80+ mins, 180+ RMB)
    • Call a taxi from the Station to Oriental Land Hotel.

Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station

  • Metro (1 hour, 6 RMB)
    • Take Metro Line 17 to its terminal station, Oriental Land station.
  • Taxi (50+ mins, 130+ RMB)
    • Call a taxi from the Station to Oriental Land Hotel.
